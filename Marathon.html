December 14th, 2025

At the end of 2024, I made it a goal to run a half marathon before my birthday. On a sunny Sunday morning right before I turned 23, I completed 13.1 miles! With 13 races total in the span of 2025, I had to end it with a big one. With the starting line a few blocks from my front door, and the race path following my local driving routes. I am excited to be running the Honolulu Marathon!
