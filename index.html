Sadie's First Marathon!


Honolulu Marathon

December 14th, 2025


On a November night out, I decided that I would run a half marathon before my birthday. Many miles, a few races, and a lot of excuses later... Days before my 23rd birthday, I completed 13.1 Miles at the Hapalua Half Marathon! While I should've stopped there, I had already invested in shoes, a Garmin watch, a lot more races, and spent an exceptional amount of time running... So why wouldn't I run the Honlulu Marathon?!? Whether you're near or far, I hope to feel your cheers!
From a message in my ears on race day, a kudos on strava, or your favorite song blasting in my ears... I will take all the help you're willing to give me to get through the hot and humid, 26.2 Miles!! Please check out all the links below to follow along in my Honolulu Marathon Journey!!!
